Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin recieved over 6 inches of rain on Friday evening. This is a record down fall for the community. Rain came down for aproximately 3 hours causing flash flood warnings and run off concerns. Some sections of town were closed due to backed up drainage systems and flooded streets. No injuries have been reported.
